Let's play COWS and BULLS!

In the game of cows and bulls, your goal is to guess a 4-digit random number. Enter one number per box and hit "Guess". Once you guess, the system will update the number of cows and bulls for your guess.

COW: This means you guessed a correct number, but it's not in the right position of the random number. Move your numbers around within the guess boxes.
BULL: This means you guessed a correct number, in the correct position!

Once you guess the correct number, the system will alert you and you can choose "New Game" if you'd like to start a new game. At that time, a new code will be generated for you to guess.
